About Me

Hi, I’m Fauzi de Ungaro, a solo videomaker & photographer.

I like to keep things simple. We talk about the idea and your project, we set the filming and delivery dates, and finally, I deliver the project ready to upload to your social media.

It could be real estate, cooking, or music projects. It could be a 1-minute or 1-hour video. I can be just the colorist for your project or the producer. The project will be ready.

Services

  • Photography & Videography
  • Live Events & Performances
  • Narrative and Creative Projects

I offer end-to-end video production as a dedicated one-person team. From concept development and location scouting to filming, editing, and color grading, I oversee every stage of the process. The result is a high-quality, ready-to-share video designed to engage audiences and perform on social media.
